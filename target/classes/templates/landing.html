Everything You Need

Powerful features,
beautifully simple.

Everything your finance team needs to manage expenses end-to-end — from submission to reimbursement.

Smart Expense Tracking

Log expenses with receipts, categories, and custom types. Full audit trail from creation to approval.

Multi-Level Approvals

Configurable workflows for managers and admins. Approve or reject with comments in one click.

Budget Management

Set monthly category limits with real-time spend tracking. Get alerts before you overspend.

Detailed Reports

Generate expense reports with PDF and CSV export. Filter by date, category, status, and more.

Real-Time Notifications

Instant in-app and email alerts for approvals, rejections, and budget warnings. Never miss an update.

Role-Based Access

Individual, Employee, Manager, and Admin roles — each with precisely scoped permissions and views.

Process

From submission to
reimbursement in minutes.

1

Submit

Employee logs an expense with amount, receipt, and category. Saved as draft or submitted instantly.

2

Review

Manager receives a notification and reviews the submitted expense with full detail view.

3

Approve

One click to approve or reject with reason. Employee is notified immediately by email and in-app.

4

Report

Admin exports full expense reports in PDF or CSV for accounting and compliance.

Access Levels

Built for every
member of your team.

Four distinct roles with tailored dashboards and permissions — from solo freelancers to enterprise admins.

Individual

For freelancers and personal finance tracking.

  • Submit & track expenses
  • Set personal budgets
  • Export own records

Employee

Company staff submitting work expenses.

  • Submit for approval
  • Track reimbursements
  • Budget visibility

Manager

Team leads who review and approve expenses.

  • Approve / reject expenses
  • Generate team reports
  • Export CSV & PDF

Admin

Full control over users and company data.

  • Manage all users
  • Audit logs access
  • Company configuration
